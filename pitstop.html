Tyre 1/4

Drag the alignment ring over the wheel (or nudge it with the arrow keys), then hit the right gun — unwind (or Z) takes the old wheel off, wind (or X) locks the fresh one on. Four tyres against the stopwatch. Fire off-centre and you cross-thread the nut; grab the wrong gun and the stop is ruined too. Pro crews get a third of the amateur tolerance.
